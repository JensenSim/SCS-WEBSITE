Kaiser De Mort

ABOUT

Kaiser De Mort is a calculated technical fighter who blends precision with a willingness to use underhanded tactics when the situation demands it. He first rose to prominence in Season 2 by capturing the X Division Title, establishing himself as a dangerous presence in the ring. Season 3 saw him collide with Phil in a fierce rivalry that unexpectedly evolved into a strong alliance as the two united against the growing threat of Rage. After defeating Rage and parting ways, KDM entered a bitter feud with his former factionmate and past SCS Champion Austin Graves. Their battles stretched across weeks, with KDM frequently relying on support from his new allies in The Empire to tilt the odds in his favor. The conflict reached its peak at Supremacy Night 1 in a brutal main event where Austin Graves ultimately prevailed.
